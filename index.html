Frequently Asked Questions

What types of files can I upload?

Upload MP3, WAV, MP4, M4A audio/video files or paste YouTube, Twitter/X, Facebook, podcast URLs. We support 50+ platforms including cloud storage links from S3, Cloudflare R2, and Cloudinary.

How accurate is the AI transcription?

Our AI achieves 97% accuracy with speaker detection. We use advanced Whisper AI models for precise speech-to-text conversion, supporting multiple languages and accents.

Can I summarize YouTube videos automatically?

Yes! Simply paste any YouTube, Vimeo, or video platform link. Our AI will automatically transcribe, summarize, and extract key points with timestamps. No downloads needed.

Is my meeting data secure and private?

All data is encrypted end-to-end, access-controlled, and never shared with third parties. We follow GDPR-compliant privacy practices. Your files are processed securely.

How long does processing take?

Most files process in 2-5 minutes depending on length. YouTube links typically take 1-2 minutes. For 60-minute meetings, expect 3-4 minute processing time.

What languages does Summarizer Labs support?

We support 50+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and Hindi. The AI automatically detects the language.
